- **Hybrid Pipeline Orchestration**: Routes requests through a Python-first deterministic engine (`hybrid_pipeline.py`) for objective scoring (skills, gaps, education) before triggering a single, backgrounded LLM call for narrative explainability, ensuring low-latency responses even if the LLM times out.
- **Shared State & Caching**: Uses a database-backed `JdCache` to share parsed Job Description structures across concurrent worker processes, while maintaining in-memory LLM singletons with keep-alive strategies to minimize cold-start latency.
- **Security Boundary Enforcement**: Intercepts all resume inputs via a centralized PII redaction service (Presidio/regex fallback) before they reach any analysis node, ensuring bias mitigation and compliance at the architectural entry point.
- **Multi-Tenant Isolation**: Enforces tenant-specific quota locks, token budgets, and storage limits at the route level, preventing resource contention and ensuring data segregation across shared infrastructure.
